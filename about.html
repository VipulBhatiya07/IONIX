Our Journey

2020
IONIX Founded

Started as a small online store with a passion for tech and customer service. Opened our first retail location in Rajkot with 200+ products.

2021
1st Milestone — 10K Customers

Expanded product range to 1,500+ items. Launched 24/7 customer support and introduced our 2-year warranty program.

2022
National Presence

Reached 30,000+ customers across India. Partnered with major brands and launched our mobile app for seamless shopping.

2023
50K Customer Milestone

Achieved 50,000+ satisfied customers and 5,000+ product catalog. Became India's most trusted tech retailer with 4.8★ rating.

2024
Global Expansion

Plans underway for international shipping and regional warehouses. Investing in AI-powered customer support and sustainability initiatives.
